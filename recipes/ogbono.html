Ogbono Soup(Draw Soup)

Ogbono Soup is also known as as Draw Soup. This soup is majorly eaten with fufu as it makes fufu recipes so easy because of it's slimmy nature which helps the lumps of fufu slide down.

Things to do before you start cooking:

  1. Use a dry mill or any substitute to dry it.
  2. Cook your meat with stock cubes pepper and onion.
  3. Cut your spinach.
  4. Grind your crayfish and pepper.
  5. Boil some water and set aside.

List of ingredients required for this soup

  1. Meat and fish: Stockfish, shaki, dry fish etc
  2. 2 handfulof ogbono seeds.
  3. 3-4 cooking soups of palm oil.
  4. 2 tablespoons of grinded cray fish.
  5. Salt and pepper.
  6. 1 onion
  7. Vegetables: Spinach, pumpkin or bitterleaf.
  8. 2 seasoning cubes.

Cooking Directions for Ogbono Soup

  1. Pour your palm oil into your pot to melt. Don't allow to heat up.
  2. You can now add your ogbono.
  3. Dissolve the ogbono in the oil, using your cooking spoon.
  4. Add your fish/ meat water when all the powder has completely mixed with oil.
  5. Set the heat of your cooker to low and start stirring. it will start to thicken and draw.
  6. Stir till the ogbono has completely absobed the meat stock.
  7. Make the ogbono absorb water by adding a small quantity of the hot water. Do this repeatedly until you get consistency.
  8. Once it starts to simmer, stir every 2-3 minutes for 20 minutes.
  9. After 20 minutes you should have started perceiving the nice aroma of the ogbono.
  10. Add your meat,fish, crayfish,and salt to give it taste. Cover and cook till the contents of the pot is well heated.
  11. Add your vegetables and stir very well. Leave to stand for about 5 minutes
  12. Serve with your fufu, garri or semolina while hot.
