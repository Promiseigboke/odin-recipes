Ora(Oha) Soup

Ora soup is a native South Eastern Nigerian soup. it is a traditional soup is similar to bitter leaf soup, the difference is that it is cooked with Ora leaves. Ora leaves are seasonal leaves.

Ingredients:-

  • Beef or your preferred protein.
  • Stock fish
  • Ground crayfish
  • Palm oil
  • Onions
  • Ponmo
  • Cocoyam
  • Oha leaves
  • seasoning
  • Water
  • Uziza leaves(optional)

How To Make Oha Soup

Put meat or fish into a pot. Add seasoning and onions. let it simmer for 5 minutes and let it cook till the meat is tender.

You can add the cocoyam now to save time.

Add 2 cups of water with palm oil, allow it to cook for 10 minutes.

Add blended cocoyam paste

Let it cook for 1 minute, then turn off the cooker.

Your delicious Oha soup is ready

Serve the ora soup with your preferred swallow(Fufu, wheat, eba, pounded yam)
