Nigerian Fried Rice Recipe

Nigerian Fried Rice is a very delicious recipe. it is also very simple to make. This Fried rice is entirely different from the Aian chicken Type of Fried Rice. This is because the main taste in the latter comes from the soy sauce while the main taste in the former comes from Seasoning cubes, Curry powder, Thyme, and of course of the availability of sufficient vegetables like Green beans, Carrots, Peas, sweet corn, and Onions.

This is a very simple recipe, but the impact it makes is quite remarkable!

For this recipe you should first of all parboil your rice, so to make the whole process easier.

Some Notes On How To Make Nigerian Fried Rice:

  1. If you like some heat, you can add cayenne pepper
  2. Do not over boil your Rice before frying it.
  3. Don't overcook the veggies, so they can still remain crunchy.
  4. Veggies like sweet bell pepper can also be used.
  5. Turn up the heat to high during the frying process as you stir continuosly
  6. You can remove the beef liver, if you don't like it.
  7. It is best to use chicken stock or Turkey stck because of their mild taste. The beef stock should be avioded as it can overpower the taste of the fried rice.

here is my very delicious and simple way of making Fried Rice.

Enjoy!

Ingredients:-

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup of chicken stock
  • 3-4 tbsp of oil for frying
  • 1 cup of diced onion
  • 1 cup of mixed Vegetables
  • 1/2 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp Curry powder
  • 2 scallions diced
  • 1 chicken stock cube
  • 1 cup beef liver
  • salt to taste
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
